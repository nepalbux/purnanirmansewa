About Purna Nirma Sewa

Founded in 2010, Purna Nirma Sewa has established itself as Pokhara's premier construction company, specializing in creating beautiful homes that harmonize with the natural beauty of the Annapurna region.

Our team combines traditional Nepali architectural elements with modern construction techniques to build homes that are both aesthetically pleasing and structurally sound, designed to withstand the test of time.

Our Construction Process

We follow a systematic approach to ensure quality and efficiency in every project we undertake.

1. Consultation & Planning

We begin with a detailed consultation to understand your vision, requirements, and budget constraints.

2. Design & Approvals

Our architects create detailed designs and secure all necessary permits and approvals from local authorities.

3. Site Preparation

We prepare the construction site, including land clearing, leveling, and establishing proper drainage systems.

4. Construction

Our skilled team executes the construction process with precision, following the approved plans and quality standards.

5. Finishing & Quality Check

We complete all interior and exterior finishing work and conduct thorough quality inspections before handover.

6. Handover & Support

We hand over your completed project and provide ongoing maintenance and support services as needed.
